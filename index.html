Welcome to CAVITRD

Supporting children with congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities in Tanzania.

About Us

CAVITRD (Congenital Anomalies, Vision Impairment, and Trauma Related Disabilities) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children in rural Tanzania. We provide surgical care, raise awareness, and promote inclusion for children born with or affected by physical disabilities.

Our Vision

To ensure every child affected by congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities receives access to surgical treatment, care, and the opportunity to thrive in a supportive and inclusive community.
